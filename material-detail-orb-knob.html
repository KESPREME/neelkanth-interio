Orb Spherical Knob

Finish: Brushed Brass | Type: Cabinet Knob

Simple yet sophisticated, the Orb knob features a perfect spherical shape that adds a touch of modern elegance to any cabinetry.

Made from solid brass with a warm, brushed brass finish, it offers a timeless look that complements various design styles, from mid-century modern to contemporary.

Specifications:

  • Material: Solid Brass
  • Finish: Brushed Brass (Also available in Matte Black, Polished Chrome)
  • Diameter: 30mm
  • Projection: 30mm
  • Included Hardware: Mounting screw (M4)
