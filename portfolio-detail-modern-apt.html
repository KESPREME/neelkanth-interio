Modern City Apartment

Style: Modern | Room: Living Room, Kitchen | Location: Cityville Center

Project Overview

This project involved transforming a standard city apartment into a sleek, modern oasis for a young professional couple. The goal was to maximize space, incorporate high-end finishes, and create a sophisticated yet comfortable atmosphere.

We focused on clean lines, a neutral color palette with bold accents, and smart storage solutions. Custom millwork in the kitchen and living area adds a bespoke touch, while carefully selected lighting enhances the mood and functionality of each zone.

Client Brief

"We wanted a home that felt modern, uncluttered, and perfect for entertaining, but also comfortable for everyday living. Maximizing natural light and incorporating durable, quality materials was key."

Key Features

  • Open-plan living and kitchen area
  • Custom walnut cabinetry and built-ins
  • Quartz countertops and integrated appliances
  • Designer lighting fixtures
  • Neutral palette with deep blue and metallic accents
  • Smart home integration (lighting, blinds)
