Geo Lines Wallpaper

Pattern: Geometric | Colors: Gold, Navy, Cream

Add a touch of Art Deco sophistication with the Geo Lines wallpaper. Striking metallic gold lines intersect against a deep navy background, creating a dynamic and luxurious feature wall.

This non-woven wallpaper offers easy application and removal, suitable for living rooms, dining areas, or bedrooms seeking a modern, graphic statement.

Specifications:

  • Material: Non-Woven Paper
  • Finish: Matte with Metallic Lines
  • Pattern Repeat: 64cm Straight Match
  • Roll Width: 52cm
  • Roll Length: 10m
  • Application: Paste the Wall
