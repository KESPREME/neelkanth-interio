Bright Scandinavian Living

Style: Scandinavian, Minimalist | Room: Living Room | Location: Suburban Home

Project Overview

Inspired by Scandinavian design principles, this living room makeover emphasizes functionality, simplicity, and natural light. We used a light color palette, natural wood elements, and cozy textiles to create a bright, airy, and inviting space.

The furniture is practical and comfortable, with clean lines and minimal clutter. The focus is on creating a 'hygge' atmosphere – a sense of coziness, well-being, and contentment.

Client Brief

"We wanted our main living area to feel bright, open, and relaxing. We love the simplicity of Scandinavian style and wanted a space that was both beautiful and practical for our family."

Key Features

  • Light wood flooring and furniture accents
  • Neutral color scheme (whites, grays, soft blues)
  • Emphasis on natural light maximization
  • Functional, comfortable furniture with clean lines
  • Cozy textiles (wool throws, linen pillows)
  • Minimalist decor and artwork
