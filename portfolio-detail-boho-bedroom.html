Boho Chic Bedroom Retreat

Style: Boho Chic, Eclectic | Room: Bedroom | Location: Creative Loft

Project Overview

This bedroom transformation embraces the free-spirited nature of Bohemian style. We layered textures, patterns, and natural elements to create a cozy, personal, and inviting retreat.

Vintage finds were mixed with new pieces, incorporating plenty of plants, macrame details, and warm wood tones. The result is an eclectic space that feels collected over time and full of personality.

Client Brief

"I wanted my bedroom to be my sanctuary – cozy, relaxed, and filled with things I love. I'm drawn to natural materials, plants, and a slightly unconventional, artistic vibe."

Key Features

  • Layered textiles (rugs, throws, pillows)
  • Mix of furniture styles (vintage, modern)
  • Abundant indoor plants
  • Macrame wall hangings and planters
  • Warm, earthy color palette
  • Unique lighting fixtures
