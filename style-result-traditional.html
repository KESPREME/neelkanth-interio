Understanding Traditional Style

Traditional design draws inspiration from classic European decor, emphasizing order, symmetry, and timeless elegance. It creates spaces that feel comfortable, familiar, and sophisticated.

Furniture often features classic silhouettes like wingback chairs or clawfoot tubs, with rich wood tones and detailed ornamentation. Fabrics tend to be substantial, showcasing patterns like damask, florals, or paisleys in harmonious color schemes. Architectural details like crown molding and built-in cabinetry are often highlighted.

Key Characteristics:

  • Emphasis on symmetry and order
  • Classic furniture silhouettes and details
  • Rich wood tones (mahogany, cherry, walnut)
  • Harmonious color palettes, often including deep hues
  • Use of patterned fabrics (floral, damask, plaid)
  • Elegant window treatments and accessories
