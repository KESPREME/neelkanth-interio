Calacatta Gold Porcelain Tile

Type: Tile | Style: Marble Look

Capture the luxurious beauty of Italian Calacatta Gold marble with the practicality and durability of porcelain. This large-format tile features dramatic grey veining accented with warm golden hues on a luminous white background.

Ideal for creating elegant floors, stunning feature walls, shower surrounds, and backsplashes in both classic and contemporary interiors.

Specifications:

  • Material: Porcelain
  • Finish: Polished (Also available in Matte)
  • Size: 600mm x 1200mm (Also available in 600x600, 300x600)
  • Thickness: 10mm
  • Edge: Rectified
  • Application: Floor & Wall (Interior)
  • PEI Rating: 4 (Moderate to Heavy Traffic)
