Artificial Vertical Garden Panel

Type: Decorative Panel | Style: Greenery

Instantly bring life and vibrant texture to any interior wall with these realistic artificial vertical garden panels. Featuring a lush mix of foliage, they create a stunning biophilic focal point without the need for maintenance.

Easy to install and connect, these panels are perfect for enhancing reception areas, living room feature walls, office spaces, or balconies, adding a touch of nature indoors.

Specifications:

  • Material: High-Quality PE (Polyethylene)
  • Foliage Type: Mixed Ferns, Boxwood, Grasses (Varies by model)
  • Panel Size: 50cm x 50cm (Approx.)
  • Backing: Flexible Grid Mesh
  • Installation: Cable ties, screws, or adhesive
  • Features: UV Resistant (Indoor Rated), Maintenance-Free
